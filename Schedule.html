About SDMAINDIA Academy

At SDMAINDIA ACADEMY, we believe in shaping discipline, strength, and focus through well-structured training schedules. Our programs are designed to fit every individual’s routine, whether you’re looking for weekday training or an intensive 6-day practice schedule.

We provide professional coaching, modern facilities, and a motivating environment where students can train, grow, and achieve their fitness and martial arts goals. With multiple class schedules, we ensure flexibility and accessibility for all our members.

Our Training Schedule:

  • 2 Days a week – Beginners & casual learners
  • 3 Days a week – Balanced training & progress
  • 6 Days a week – Intensive & advanced programs
